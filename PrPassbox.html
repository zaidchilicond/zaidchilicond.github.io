PASSBOX

ELECTRICAL INTERLOCK FOR CRITICAL ENVIRONMENT

Pass Box is supporting equipment for clean room and pharmaceutical manufacturing facilities and laboratories it can be used for passing the wares between clean room to clean room or clean room to non-clean rooms door and minimize the contamination extent of clean area.

Body of the cases is made of stainless steel grade 304 by folding and welding and its surface is treated with the interlock function, electrical interlock.
